We use essential cookies for authentication and session management.

4. Account Deletion, Data Retention, and Security Quarantine

a) Right to Delete (Account Closure)

You have the right to request the deletion of your account at any time. To initiate this process, you must contact our support team.

b) Security Retention Period (1 to 2 Years)

Upon your request for deletion, your account access will be immediately blocked/deactivated. However, strictly for security purposes, fraud prevention, anti-cheat enforcement, and legal compliance, your data will remain stored on our secure servers for a period of 1 to 2 years (12 to 24 months).

c) Permanent Deletion

After the retention period expires, all personal identifiers and data associated with your account will be permanently deleted from our live databases, provided there are no active legal disputes or investigations involving the account.
