CODE OF CONDUCT – OURSTARS

Clause 41.1 — Any form of verbal aggression, hate speech, prejudice, or discrimination is strictly prohibited, including but not limited to homophobia, racism, xenophobia, harassment, threats, or any other toxic or offensive behavior against other users, OurStars STAFF members, or third parties.

Clause 41.2 — The user agrees to use the website and the game OurStars in an ethical, respectful, and lawful manner, and strictly in accordance with their intended purposes. Any conduct that disrupts the experience of other players or compromises the proper functioning of the platform is prohibited.

Clause 41.3 — The promotion, advertisement, or disclosure of other games, servers, communities, external links, products, or services without prior and explicit authorization from the OurStars STAFF is strictly prohibited.

Clause 41.4 — It is forbidden to publish, share, or disseminate pornographic content (NSFW), explicit violence (gore), illegal material, offensive content, or any material deemed inappropriate for the OurStars community.

Clause 41.5 — The use, distribution, promotion, or encouragement of cheats, hacks, exploits, intentional bugs, or any software or method that provides unfair advantages or compromises the integrity, balance, or security of the game is strictly prohibited.

Clause 41.6 — Impersonating members of the OurStars STAFF, other players, or any third party for the purpose of obtaining advantages, misleading others, or causing harm is strictly prohibited.

Clause 41.7 — The use of alternative accounts (“alts”) to bypass punishments, restrictions, or limitations is prohibited. Any attempt to hack, manipulate, or exploit the system to gain unfair advantages is also forbidden.

Clause 41.8 — Nicknames, usernames, characters, or avatars that reference pornography, profanity, hate speech, discrimination, or offensive content are not permitted.

Clause 41.9 — Spam, flooding, repetitive messages, or any behavior that disrupts communication, navigation, gameplay, or the organization of the website or game is prohibited.

Clause 41.10 — Insults, provocations, harassment, disrespect, or attempts to discredit or undermine the OurStars STAFF will not be tolerated. All decisions made by the STAFF must be respected.

Clause 41.11 — The disclosure of personal information, whether your own or that of third parties, is strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to, phone numbers, email addresses, passwords, or any other sensitive data. Any attempt to violate the privacy, security, or integrity of users or the platform is forbidden.

Clause 41.12 — Sharing, requesting, soliciting, or attempting to obtain passwords, account emails, authentication data, or any form of account access from other users is strictly prohibited. Violation of this clause will result in a permanent account ban, without the right to any refund.

Clause 41.13 — Stalking, persistent harassment, suppression or falsification of evidence, and the creation of false or malicious reports are strictly prohibited. Such actions will result in a permanent ban and may be reported to the competent regional authorities.

Clause 41.14 — Any illegal, immoral, abusive, or inappropriate activity, as well as any conduct that violates local, national, or international laws and regulations, is strictly prohibited.

Clause 41.15 — Any activity deemed illegal or inappropriate may result in a permanent account ban for breach of contract, without the right to a refund.

Clause 41.16 — Failure to comply with these clauses may result, at the sole discretion of the OurStars STAFF, in warnings, temporary suspensions, permanent account bans, or deletion of associated data.

Clause 41.17 — Ignorance of these rules or clauses does not exempt the user from penalties or sanctions.

Clause 41.18 — The OurStars STAFF reserves the right to modify, update, or revise these rules at any time, aiming to ensure continuous improvement, security, fairness, and balance of the website and the game.

Clause 41.19 — Situations not expressly covered by these clauses will be analyzed and resolved exclusively by the OurStars STAFF, based on common sense, fairness, and the preservation of the community.

Additional Provision — The user is subject to all rules, guidelines, and obligations established in the OurStars Terms of Use, even if not explicitly mentioned in this Code of Conduct.

By accessing or using the website or the game, the user declares that they have read, understood, and agreed to all of the conditions described herein. For full and detailed information, users are encouraged to read the complete Terms of Use.

Final Provision — Any violation of these rules shall be considered a breach of contract.

Depending on the severity of the violation, the user's account may be subject to a temporary suspension or a permanent ban, at the sole discretion of the OurStars STAFF.
