CODE OF CONDUCT – OURSTARS (Updated 2024/2025)

Clause 41.1 – Zero Tolerance for Discrimination: Any form of verbal aggression, hate speech, or discrimination is strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to, attacks based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, or age. OurStars maintains a zero-tolerance policy for toxic behavior against users, STAFF members, or third parties.

Clause 41.2 – Ethical Use and Citizenship: Users agree to interact with the OurStars platform and game in an ethical, respectful, and lawful manner. Any conduct intended to disrupt the experience of other players, incite toxicity, or compromise the social harmony of the platform is strictly forbidden.

Clause 41.3 – Unauthorized Commercial Activity and Promotion: The promotion, advertisement, or solicitation of external games, servers, communities, products, or services is prohibited without prior written authorization from OurStars STAFF. This includes "referral link" spam and unauthorized commercial recruitment.

Clause 41.4 – Inappropriate Content (NSFW): Disseminating, sharing, or referencing pornographic content (NSFW), explicit violence (gore), illegal substances, or any material deemed disturbing or inappropriate for a general audience is strictly prohibited.

Clause 41.5 – Integrity and Fair Play: The use, development, distribution, or promotion of cheats, hacks, scripts, bots, or unauthorized third-party software that automates gameplay or provides unfair advantages is prohibited. Exploiting "bugs" or unintended game mechanics for gain instead of reporting them to STAFF is considered a violation of integrity.

Clause 41.6 – Impersonation and False Identity: Impersonating OurStars STAFF, other players, influencers, or public figures to mislead, defraud, or gain advantages is strictly prohibited and subject to immediate permanent suspension.

Clause 41.7 – Account Integrity and Evasion: The use of alternative accounts ("alts") to bypass active bans, restrictions, or platform limitations is forbidden. Any attempt to manipulate the system to circumvent security measures will result in the suspension of all associated accounts.

Clause 41.8 – Naming Policy (Nicknames and Avatars): Usernames, guild names, or avatars that contain profanity, hate speech, sexual references, or are designed to mock other users/STAFF are not permitted and will be changed or banned upon discovery.

Clause 41.9 – Communication Standards (Spam and Flooding): Disrupting public communication channels through spam, flooding (repetitive text), excessive use of caps, or any behavior that hinders the ability of others to communicate or navigate the platform is prohibited.

Clause 41.10 – Interaction with STAFF: Harassment, insults, or attempts to discredit OurStars STAFF members are not tolerated. STAFF decisions regarding moderation and game balance are final. Appeals must be handled through the official support channels in a respectful manner.

Clause 41.11 – Privacy and Doxing (PII): Disclosing, or threatening to disclose, the personal information of any user or STAFF member (address, real name, IP, phone, etc.) is a severe violation. Any attempt to compromise the privacy and safety of others will be reported to the relevant legal authorities.

Clause 41.12 – Account Security and Phishing: Soliciting or attempting to obtain login credentials, authentication tokens, or sensitive data from other users is strictly prohibited. This includes "social engineering" tactics. Violation results in a permanent ban with no possibility of appeal.

Clause 41.13 – Malicious Reporting and Harassment: Stalking, persistent harassment (cyberbullying), and the creation of false or malicious reports against other users to trigger automated bans are prohibited. Abuse of the reporting system is subject to the same penalties as the offenses being falsely reported.

Clause 41.14 – Real Money Trading (RMT): The sale or purchase of accounts, in-game items, or currency for "real-world" money or external assets is strictly prohibited unless facilitated by an official platform feature. This protects users from fraud and maintains game balance.

Clause 41.15 – Legal Compliance: Any activity that violates local, national, or international laws—including but not limited to copyright infringement, cybercrimes, or threats of physical violence—is strictly prohibited and will result in a permanent termination of service.

Clause 41.16 – Sanctions and Enforcement: Failure to comply with this Code of Conduct may result in warnings, temporary mutes, temporary suspensions, or permanent account bans, at the sole discretion of the OurStars STAFF. All virtual assets associated with a banned account are forfeited.

Clause 41.17 – Responsibility of Awareness: Claiming ignorance of these rules does not exempt the user from penalties. It is the user's responsibility to stay informed about the Code of Conduct and Terms of Use.

Clause 41.18 – Updates and Revisions: OurStars STAFF reserves the right to modify or update these clauses at any time to reflect the evolution of the community and platform. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new terms.

Clause 41.19 – Discretionary Resolution: Situations not explicitly covered by these clauses will be resolved by the OurStars STAFF based on the principles of fairness, community safety, and the "spirit of the game."

Additional Provision: This Code of Conduct is an extension of the OurStars Terms of Use. In case of conflict, the Terms of Use shall prevail.

Final Provision: Any violation of these rules constitutes a material breach of contract. Depending on the severity, OurStars STAFF reserves the right to terminate access permanently without prior notice or refund of any invested values.
